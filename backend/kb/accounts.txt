Savings Account

A savings account allows customers to deposit money securely while earning interest.

Required documents:
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport Size Photograph
- Address Proof

Customers can open a savings account online or by visiting the nearest branch.

Minimum balance depends on the account type.

KYC verification is mandatory before activation.

A debit card and cheque book may be issued after account opening.
